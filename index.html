About Swift Type

Experience the thrill of typing a series of quotes or sentences within a specified time limit.

Measure your performance in terms of Characters per Minute (CPM), Words per Minute (WPM), accuracy, and errors.

How It Works

1. Click the "Start" button to ignite the challenge.

2. Type the given text within the allocated time.

3. Witness your accuracy as incorrectly typed characters are marked. Remember, you can't go back or type more characters than provided.

4. Upon completion, your performance will be displayed.
